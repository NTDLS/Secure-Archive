Self Extractor - How to Use



Using a self-extractor is very straight forward - As the name implies. The primary intention is to allow third parties to easily decrypt data from these archives - however, those who do have Secure Archive installed can right click on Self-Extractors and easily add/remove and browse through its contents (if they have the proper password).

The steps for extracting data without Secure Archive installed are as follows:
Steps:

  1. Double click on the Self-Extractor.
  2. If it is encrypted, you will be prompted for a password (figure 1).
  3. You will now be prompted for a destination location to decrypt the files to. (figure 2).
  4. A status window will keep you up-to-date as your files are unpacked (figure 3)..
