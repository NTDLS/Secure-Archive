Self Extractor - How to Create



You should read and understand the Encryption Walk through and Decryption Walk through before continuing, as this section builds on the information obtained there.

Creating a self-extractor is actually quite easy. Once you have created an encrypted archive (see the Encryption Walk through), you can simply right click on the resulting file, then select "Create Self-Extractor" from the Secure Archive sub-menu as demonstrated below.
Steps:

  1. Encrypt / Archive your files.
  2. Click the resulting encrypted archive with the right mouse button.
  3. Navigate to the pop-up menu sub-item called "Secure Archive" and select "Create Self-Extractor" (figure 1).
  4. At this point you will see a progress window as your self extractor is created (figure 2).
